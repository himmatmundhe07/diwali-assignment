FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQS)

  • Can Cetaphil cleansers be used daily? +

    Yes, all Cetaphil cleansers are formulated to be gentle enough for daily use, even on sensitive skin. They effectively cleanse without stripping the skin of its natural oils or disrupting its moisture barrier.

  • Are Cetaphil cleansers suitable for acne-prone skin? +

    Many of our cleansers are excellent for acne-prone skin as they are non-comedogenic (won't clog pores) and gentle. We also offer specific formulations, like the Gentle Exfoliating SA Cleanser, designed to target concerns associated with acne-prone skin.

  • Can I use a facial cleanser as a body cleanser? +

    While our facial cleansers are gentle, we recommend using products specifically designed for the body, as the skin on your body has different needs. However, products like the original Gentle Skin Cleanser are formulated to be versatile and can be used on both face and body.
